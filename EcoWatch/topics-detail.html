Illegal Poaching Prevention: By leveraging satellite data and advanced analytics, EcoWatch has successfully detected and prevented numerous instances of illegal poaching in marine protected areas. Coral Reef Restoration: Through our coral reef health monitoring efforts, EcoWatch has contributed to the restoration and preservation of coral reefs worldwide. Pollution Mitigation: EcoWatch's early detection systems have played a crucial role in identifying and addressing pollution hotspots in marine environments.
