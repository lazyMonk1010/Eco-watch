How does it work?



  • Data Collection, processing and analysis

    EcoWatch casts a watchful eye through a network of underwater cameras, sensors, and satellites, collecting a wealth of real-time data on marine life, water conditions, and even ship movements.The raw data is just the beginning. Advanced algorithms and artificial intelligence go to work, analyzing vast amounts of information. Image recognition identifies illegal fishing activities and tracks coral health indicators, while time series analysis detects subtle shifts in sensor data, uncovering potential pollution threats before they escalate.

  • Actionable Insights & Transparency

    EcoWatch doesn't just collect data; it acts upon it. Authorities receive immediate alerts of suspicious activities, allowing them to swiftly intervene. Scientists gain access to dedicated dashboards, monitoring coral health trends and identifying areas needing urgent protection also the anonymized data is made available for research and public education, fostering transparency and building trust.

  • Continuous Learning & Improvement

    EcoWatch is a living system, constantly evolving. Machine learning models refine their accuracy with every new data point, becoming adept at identifying even the most subtle threats. User feedback and scientific validation further optimize the system's effectiveness, ensuring it remains a powerful tool for ocean protection.

Who are we ?

At EcoWatch, we are committed to leveraging cutting-edge technology to address threats to marine life. Our goal is to develop innovative solutions for illegal fishing detection, coral reef health monitoring, and early pollution detection.

Our team consists of passionate individuals with expertise in marine conservation, technology, and environmental science. Together, we are working towards a common goal of protecting and preserving our oceans.

At EcoWatch, we're dedicated to making a profound impact on marine conservation. Through innovative technology and collaborative partnerships, we prevent illegal poaching, restore coral reefs, and mitigate pollution hotspots. Engaging communities globally, we empower individuals through citizen science initiatives and advocate for sustainable policies. By fostering international cooperation and leveraging data-driven evaluation, we continuously strive for a brighter future for our oceans, ensuring their health and vitality for generations to come.
